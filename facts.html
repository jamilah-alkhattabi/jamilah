What does the name Cappadocia mean?

Cappadocia takes its name from the Persian phrase Katpaktukya, which means "the land of beautiful horses," as the region was noted for its equestrian offerings in ancient times.

Turkey is a popular destination for tourist, it  is a gorgeous country with a diverse range of attractions and a rich cultural heritage.  And tourism is one of the most dynamic and rapidly expanding economic sectors in Turkey. This is why the Covid-19 pandamic had a significant impact on the Turkish economy due to border closures. The graph below shows the number of foreign tourists who visited Turkey from the beginning of the pandamic until April 2023.

According to Istanbul's Provincial Directorate of Culture and Tourism, after COVID-19 in 2022, the number of foreign tourists has begun to rise again, with nearly 6.7 million arrivals in July 2022. In April 2023, this figure is expected to exceed 3.3 million.
