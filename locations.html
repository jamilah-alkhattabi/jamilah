Things to Do in Cappadocia:

1. Hot Air Balloon

Cappadocia is one of the most popular destinations in the world for hot air ballooning, due to its magical landscapes. Flights can range from 45 minutes to three hours. It depends on how you book your tour and the flight deal you choose.Direct Online Booking.

2. Hiking

Hiking gives you the opportunity to discover hidden treasures.I will list the top five hiking routes in Cappadocia, ranked from easiest to most difficult:

  • Pigeon Valley
  • Love Valley and White Valley
  • Rose Valley
  • Red Valley
  • Ihlara Valley

    • 3. Horse Riding

    Horses have been at the heart of the Cappadocian economy throughout its history, from the Persians who used them as payment to the Romans who exported them back for chariot racing. They can access locations that a tour bus cannot, and the scenery is great for horseback riding or horse safari.

    4. Stay in The Cave Hotel
